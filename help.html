CarryOver

CarryOver is a smart "Read Later" and content collection app that seamlessly integrates with your iOS or macOS workflow. It allows you to save text and URLs from any app and access them later, with built-in iCloud sync to keep your data available across all your devices.

Features

  • Universal Sharing: Share text and links from any app using the system Share Sheet.
  • Smart URL Detection: Automatically identifies URLs in shared text and provides a one-tap button to open them.
  • iCloud Sync: Your saved items are automatically synced across all your devices using CloudKit.
  • History Tracking: Keep track of your reading history.
  • Pro Features: Unlock unlimited daily saves (Free tier has a daily limit).

How to Use

1. Saving Content

  1. Open any app (e.g., Safari, Twitter, Notes).
  2. Select the text or URL you want to save.
  3. Tap the Share button.
  4. Select CarryOver from the list of apps.
    • Note: If you don't see CarryOver, tap "More..." and enable it.
  5. The content will be saved to your list automatically.

2. Managing Your List

  • View Items: Open the CarryOver app to see your "Recent" list.
  • Open Links: If an item contains a URL, tap the "Open Link" button to view it immediately.
  • Delete Items: Swipe left on an item to delete it.
  • Retry Sync: If an item fails to sync (indicated by a red icon), tap the icon to retry.

3. History

  • Tap the History button (clock icon) at the bottom of the list to view your archived items.
